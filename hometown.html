Uberlandia, MG, Brazil

City Information

Uberlandia is a city located in the Brazilian state of Minas Gerais. It has a population of approximately 700,000 people and is known for its agricultural industry, as well as its burgeoning technology sector. Uberlandia is also home to the Universidade Federal de Uberlândia, a well-respected public university.

The city has a rich cultural scene, with various museums, theatres, and libraries that you can visit during your stay. Also, Uberlandia has many options for those who like to walk and do exercises like parks, squares, and bike paths.

Things to do in Uberlandia

If you're looking for something to eat, be sure to try the local specialty dish called "pão de queijo" which is a small cheese roll.
